Hello and welcome to my website!

My name is Son. I am a software developer at DearLucy - a start-up located in Helsinki, Finland. We build Business Intelligence dashboards which provide accurate key performance indicator (KPI) data in real time and in transpent manner. This helps leaders to monitor business performance and communicate easily with organization and interest groups.

I graduated from Lahti University of Applied Sciences. My major is Business Information Technology which is a hybrid degree program of Business and IT, as its name implies. However, I focused more on IT and spend most of my studies time working on it.

I am a supporter of the open-source movement. It is an effective means for learning and improving as well as sharing knowledge with other people.

My favourite dev environment and tools are Linux with i3wm, zsh, tmux, git, and vim or VSCode with vim keybindings.
